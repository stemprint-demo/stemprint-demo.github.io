StemPrint Demo

Demo page for the paper "StemPrint: Stem-Aware Audio Fingerprinting for Automatic Sample Identification." This page presents qualitative retrieval cases with ranking results, model summaries, and selected query-reference audio segments.

Overview

StemPrint separates music into source stems and compares stem-level fingerprints to improve track-level sample retrieval. Each case shows ranking and summary figures side by side, followed by audio segments selected from the highest-scoring query-reference segment pair for each model.

Author information is omitted during the review stage.
